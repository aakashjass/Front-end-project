About Us

Welcome to The Brainbelt School of Architecture

The Brainbelt school of architecture, is the outcome of the vision and initiative of the founder, S.Sundararajan, B.E., as an ideal Educationalist and Philanthropist, he has been devoting his full time for the Upliftment of backward classes in the area of higher technical Education. This Institute has started in the year June 2000, established in rural area near Chennai city. This Institution is one among the several professional institutions run by Kalasalingam Charities with the background of successfully running professional institutions for more than 30 years. The institution is located in a sprawling area of 25 acres in Kalasalingam Nagar, IT Corridor, Rajiv Gandhi Salai (OMR), and 15 Kilometers from Chennai City.
